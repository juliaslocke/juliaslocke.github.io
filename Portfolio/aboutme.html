A little bit about me...

Hi! My name is Julia Locke, and I appreciate you taking the time to learn a little more about me. Born and raised in Livingston, NJ I am a current senior at the University of Michigan pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information in the School of Information with a focus on User Experience. Even before applying to the School of Information, I knew that User Experience was a field that I could not only find success in but also enjoy. I am a dedicated and hard working student, and am ready and excited to apply this mindset to a career.

If you noticed, on my homepage, I wrote out my favorite quote. I love to read, and four summers ago I chose The Last Lecture for my summer reading project. While many may have focused on the sad backstory that led author Randy Pausch to write the book, I was instead inspired by his positivity and outlook on life. I now try to live life with the same positivity and "bright side" mentality.
