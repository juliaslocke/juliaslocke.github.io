Agile Development Tools

While working as an UX Intern, I had the responsibility of designing materials to be used in assessments of Agile Development teams. The Agile Development teams were implementing a new way of assessing the satisfaction and success of each team. This involved utilizing cards that would describe different areas of concern. The requirements for these cards were that they had to clearly present all information on the card so that they could be viewed and understood from a distance. The images also had to be simple but descriptive of the main ideas of each card.

Internal Application

While working as an UX Intern, I was part of a team that had the responsibility of brainstorming and prototyping an internal application. The application was to be used to measure employee satisfaction and capability in order to determine company productivity. The requirements were that the application had to be enjoyable for users, whether that meant quick to complete, engaging, or both, and it had to tell employees the value that would come from participation with the application.
